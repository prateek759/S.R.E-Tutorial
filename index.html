About Us

About Our Institute

S.R.E Tutorial is a trusted academic coaching centre in Varanasi, providing comprehensive coaching for students from Class I to XII across U.P., C.B.S.E., and I.C.S.E. boards.

Our approach centres on strong conceptual understanding, personal attention to every student, and regular assessments — ensuring each child reaches their full academic potential in a nurturing and disciplined environment.

  • Qualified and experienced subject teachers
  • Small batch sizes for personalised attention
  • Regular weekly tests and feedback sessions
  • Modern smart classroom with projector facilities
  • Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions every week
  • Trusted by hundreds of families in Varanasi
